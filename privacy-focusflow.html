Privacy Policy

FocusFlow - Focus Timer

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

FocusFlow ("the App") is a focus timer application for iOS. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use FocusFlow.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

All data you create in FocusFlow, including timer sessions, focus history, and settings, is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Analytics and Tracking

FocusFlow does not use any analytics services, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks. We do not track your usage, behavior, or any other activity within the App.

Third-Party Services

FocusFlow does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. The App operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection.

Data Storage and Security

All your data is stored locally on your device. It is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID. If you delete the App, all associated data will be permanently removed from your device.

Notifications

FocusFlow may request permission to send local notifications to alert you when a focus session ends. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission.

Children's Privacy

FocusFlow does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App does not collect personal information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
