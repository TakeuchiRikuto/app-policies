Privacy Policy

Keigo AI - Business Writing

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Overview

Keigo AI ("the App") is a business writing assistant for iOS that uses AI to check and improve professional communications. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use Keigo AI.

Data Collection

We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service:

  • Text submitted for checking: Text you submit is sent to the Claude AI API (provided by Anthropic) for processing. This text is not stored on our servers after processing is complete.
  • Anonymous device identifier: A non-personal device ID is used solely for rate limiting (managing free daily check allowances). This identifier cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Subscription data: If you subscribe, purchase and billing information is managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

How Your Text Is Processed

When you submit text for checking, it is sent securely (via HTTPS) to the Claude AI API for analysis. The AI processes your text and returns corrections and explanations. Your submitted text is not stored on our servers after the response is delivered to your device. We do not retain, log, or use your text for any purpose beyond providing the immediate correction service.

Third-Party Services

Keigo AI uses the following third-party service:

  • Anthropic Claude API: Used to analyze and correct your submitted text. Anthropic's data handling practices are governed by their own privacy policy, available at anthropic.com/privacy. When using the API for commercial applications, Anthropic does not use submitted data to train their models.

The App does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or other tracking tools.

Data Storage and Security

Your correction history and app settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

All network communication between the App and the AI service is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. If you delete the App, all locally stored data will be permanently removed from your device.

Children's Privacy

Keigo AI does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for business professionals and does not target children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
