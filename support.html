Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my data?

For TradeLog and FocusFlow, simply delete the app from your device -- all data is stored locally and will be removed. For MiniBoard, you can delete your account and all associated data from the app's settings, or contact us via email.

Do you collect personal information?

TradeLog and FocusFlow collect no personal data at all. MiniBoard collects only the minimum data necessary to provide community features. See each app's privacy policy for details.

How do I report inappropriate content on MiniBoard?

You can report content directly within the MiniBoard app by tapping the report option on any post or comment. You can also email us at miniboard.app.support@gmail.com.

Is my trading data in TradeLog synced or shared?

No. TradeLog stores all data exclusively on your device. Nothing is uploaded, synced, or shared with us or any third party.
